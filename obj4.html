Network Security Objective 4

Create a policy or procedure that addresses events such as:

a disaster recovery plan, a business continuity plan,

an incident response policy, an acceptable usage document,

an information security policy, a physical security policy,

assessments or troubleshooting procedures.

Objective Application

For this objective, I had two assignments from my NTS405 Incident Response
class and my NTW275 Network Infrastructure Design class that best displayed
my mastery of this objective.

In my NTS405 Incident Response class I was introduced to the steps necessary
to be well prepared as a member of an incident response team. I was taught what
role I may play in an emergency and how best to be a reliable asset for other
members of the incident response team. I was able to display what I learned
in this class with my Final Assignment.

For this Final my tasked was to write up a comprehensive Incident Response plan
on how I would war game an incident. I was to include any given disaster scenario,
the potential events that could occur this event, and how I would respond in this
scenario as a member of a incident response team.

And in NTW275 Network Infrastructure Design I learned how to translate organizational
needs into network designs. This included security considerations for the logic of
both the physical network topology and the server infrastructure in the design process.
My Final Assignment was a good showcase of what I learned in the class.

This Final had me conducting a Risk Assessment for a fictional company's office network,
then provide a recommendation in the way of a network configuration that could potentially
mitigate the risks that I had identified in my assessment.

I feel confident that I met this objective with the presentation of my incident response and risk
assessment documentation where both had the utmost adherence to industry standards and regulations.
