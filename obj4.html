Network Security Objective 4

Create a policy or procedure that addresses events such as:

a disaster recovery plan, a business continuity plan,

an incident response policy, an acceptable usage document,

an information security policy, a physical security policy,

assessments or troubleshooting procedures.

Objective Application

This particular objective was fully covered in my
NTS405 Incident Response class. In this class I was
introduced to the steps necessary to be well prepared as
a member of an incident response team. I was taught what
role I may play in an emergency and how best to be a reliable
asset for other members of the incident response team. I was able
to display what I learned in this class with my Final Assignment.

For this Final my tasked was to write up a comprehensive
Incident Response plan on how I would wargame an incident.
I was to include any given disaster scenario, the potential
events that could occur this event, and how I would respond
in this scenario as a member of a incident response team.

I feel confident that I met this objective with the presentation
of my incident response documentation and the adherance to
industry standards and regulations.
