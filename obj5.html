Network Security Objective 5

Develop a research report or implementation plan concerning

legal and ethical best practices and mandated requirements

that pertain to information security.

Objective Application

With this particular objective, I had two assignments from my
NTS435 International and Federal INFOSEC Standards and Regulations
class and my TCH310 Technology, Society and Ethics class that best
displayed my mastery of this objective.

From my NTS435 International and Federal INFOSEC Standards and Regulations
class I had an assignment to select a Security Framework from a provided list and
write a detailed research paper about the framework.

For this research paper the Security Framework I chose was FISMA,
The Federal Information Security Modernization Act of 2014. I provided
an indepth look at this framework, going into the background of the framework
with it's purpose and scope, looking at the controls and sub-controls, and
explaining the authorization and accreditation process for this framework.

With this research paper I covered the legal aspects of the objective with this government
regulation for the implementation of policies and best practices for information systems.

But because the very nature of our industry reaches beyond government regulation or
business operations, it often bleeds into the fabric of our society. The FISMA framework
was put in place to help protect the the general public. And while the government
recommends that businesses use this framework in their operations, not all adopt it
and this often leads to highly questionable ethical business practices, as best shown
in my assignment from my TCH310 Technology, Society and Ethics class.

In TCH310 Technology, Society and Ethics we studied essential concepts
necessary to evaluate the social and ethical impacts of the design,
creation and use of new technologies. This required us to carfully study
and evaluate dilemmas and scenarios that highlighted how technology
challenges social and moral principles in the modern day. One such
scenario was to identify ethical issues related to any technology.

In this assignment I was to pick a technology topic from a provided
list and identify three ethical issues related to that topic, present
them in a Powerpoint presentation and discuss this with my peers.
I chose video gaming as my topic and discussed some of the questionable
practices that were taking place. Of these issues I spoke about unethical
gambling mechanics with loot boxes targeting children, the toxic and
unsustainable work culture of crunch placed on developers, and the pushing
of mature content to young audiences.

Researching and discussing the ethics of this portion of the technology
industry helped me to understand this objective and identify the importance
of the ethical requirements that guide information security.
